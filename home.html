VISION

Our vision is to equip students for a life of integrity, faith, and impact. We accomplish that by providing a world-class, Christ-centered education that fosters creativity and instills wisdom, love, and a desire to serve.

OUR KEY OBJECTIVES

Christian Character
Education Excellence
Creative Expression
Building Community
Communication
Future Authoring
Developing students’ worldview based on a Christ-centered foundation that creates a desire to seek truth in Jesus and make disciples.
Creating critical thinkers and problem solvers who possess the knowledge and abilities to compete globally.
Promoting creativity in the pursuit of worshipping God through self-discovery and the development of talent and ability.
Creating a harmonious environment through servant leadership on campus that can extend to families, communities, and beyond.
Creating excellent communicators with the ability to speak and write with fact-based reasoning to contend with the world in an influential manner.
Focusing students on life goal development and planning to orient and author direction that will lead to the fulfillment of their God-given purpose.
