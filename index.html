Explore Our Clubs & Activities

At EIA, we offer a diverse range of clubs and extracurricular activities designed to nurture students' talents and interests. From arts and drama to technology and sports, there's something for everyone.

Music, Dance & Drama Club The Music, Dance, and Drama Club Under the inspiring leadership of Vincent and the mentorship of the esteemed music teacher, Mr. Paul, this vibrant club thrives as a hub for artistic expression.
